Scrolls: Where to Find Them and How to Use Them

Scrolls are parchment or vellum inscribed with spells, incantations, and magical formulas.

They are often rolled up and secured with ribbons or seals that protect and preserve the magic within.

These scrolls are crafted with meticulous care and enchanted to contain potent magical energies, making them indispensable for both novice and master wizards alike.

Adventurous wizards might find scrolls in hidden magical enclaves or ancient ruins.

These scrolls can be incredibly powerful but are often protected by formidable wards and traps.

Instead, Wizards can also find scrolls in many shops within wizarding towns.

To activate a scroll, a wizard must channel their magical energy into the scroll.

This is often done by touching the scroll and intoning a specific activation phrase.
